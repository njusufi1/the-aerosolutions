About AeroSolutions


At Aero Solutions, we specialize in advanced drone services for the energy and infrastructure sectors, helping our clients improve efficiency, safety, and environmental stewardship.

Our core services include:

1.⁠ ⁠Installing Bird Diverters

Protecting birds from collisions with power lines is crucial for wildlife conservation. At Aero Solutions, we provide a cutting-edge solution by installing bird diverters using drones, all without the need for power shutdowns or costly, disruptive methods. Unlike older techniques that required halting energy flow for installation, our drone technology allows us to complete the process efficiently and safely, with minimal impact on operations. This innovative approach not only saves time and resources but also ensures that transmission lines remain fully operational while safeguarding bird populations.

2.⁠ ⁠Wind Turbine Inspection

Our drone inspections provide a fast and detailed look at the condition of wind turbines, including blades and towers, without the need for manual climbing or shutdowns. We deliver precise, real-time data that helps identify potential issues early, reducing downtime and minimizing maintenance costs. With our service, you can keep your turbines running smoothly while saving on traditional inspection expenses.

3.⁠ ⁠Drone Imagery for Marketing

Capture stunning aerial footage of your projects with Aero Solutions' professional drone imagery services. From wind farms to construction sites, our high-quality visuals bring a unique perspective to your marketing campaigns. Whether for presentations, promotions, or digital platforms, our drone footage helps showcase your assets in a compelling way that sets your brand apart.
